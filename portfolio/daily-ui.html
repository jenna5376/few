Introduction

As someone new to UI design, I didn't have a lot of confidence when it came to my design skills. While looking up remedies for this, I stumbled upon the daily UI challenge, where you can sign up for newsletters to receive a new design prompt every day--except for the weekends. I decided to take on this challenge to improve my eye for UI design, learn to design quicker under time constraints, and further familiarize myself with tools like Figma and Adobe Illustrator.
